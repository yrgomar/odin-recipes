Taco


Now these tacos will keep you full for hours and are perfect for when you're on the cut.
Ingredients
  • 6 Oz of cooked protein of your choosing. (I prefer ground beef or chicken breast)
  • 2 Mission Carb Balance Tortillas
  • 1/4 cup of low fat mexican cheese
  • 1/3 cup of guacamole
  • 1/4 cup of low fat sour cream
  • 1/2 cup chopped iceberg lettuce
Steps
  1. Heat up tortillas
  2. Once tortillas are hot to the touch, place 3 oz of cooked protein on each tortilla
  3. Sprinkle 2 tablespoons of cheese on each taco
  4. Evenly place guacamole on tacos
  5. Place 2 tablespoons of sour cream on each taco
  6. Place 1/4 cup of iceberg lettuce on each taco
  7. Enjoy your high protein low calorie meal
