A Normal Tuna Sandwich


Are you ever too lazy to cook, but to broke to order delivery? This is the recipe for you!
Ingredients
  • 2 slices of butter bread
  • 1 packet of target tuna
  • 2 tablespoons of low fat mayo
  • A couple pickle slices
  • 1/4 cup of chopped iceberg lettuce
  • A little bit of hot sauce
Steps
  1. Toast your bread
  2. Put tuna and mayo in a bowl and combine
  3. Put a little hot sauce on one slice of bread and spread evenly
  4. Place pickle slices on other slice of bread
  5. Scoop tuna and place on top of bread with the pickles
  6. Place lettuce on top of tuna
  7. Place hot sauce bread on top of lettuce and complete the sandwich
  8. Serve with chips or fries and enjoy!
