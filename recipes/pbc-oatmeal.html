Peanut Butter Chocolate Oatmeal


The perfect winter bulk breakfast.
Ingredients
  • 1 packet of kodak chocolate chip protein oatmeal
  • 2 tablespoons of organic crunchy peanut butter
  • 1 tablespoon of any chocolate spread
  • 1/4 cup boiling water
Steps
  1. Put water to boil
  2. Pour packet of oatmeal into bowl
  3. Pour boiling water into bowl of oatmeal and let soak for 2-3 minutes
  4. Mix in peanut butter and chocolate spread
  5. Enjoy!
